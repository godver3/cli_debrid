Here's how the conversion process works:

  • First, we'll scan your existing Plex libraries to build a collection database
  • Then, we'll create organized symlinks for each file in your library
  • Movies and TV Shows will be automatically sorted into their respective folders
  • You will need to update your existing Plex libraries to point to the new symlinked locations
  • Future downloads will automatically follow the new symlink organization
⚠️ Please verify your symlink configuration before proceeding:
